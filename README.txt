Name your videos vid_0.mov, vid_1.mov etc all the way up to vid_127.mov
Drag your folder with the videos in it into the drop folder area in woulgvj08insta or whatever its called
Set midi output on channel 1 to IAC bus or Max midi or whatever
Put in IP address of host computer in midisend patch (localhost if you're running both on one computer).
Channel 1 controls clip selection
Channel 2 controls clip dimensions
Channel 3 controls noise mask selection
Channel 4 controls geometric mask selection
Midi CTL messages 1 and 2 control extra brightness (up to white out) and brightness attenuation (down to black) respectively.
Have fun.
